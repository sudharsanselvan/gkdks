WHO WE ARE

A small studio, built on purpose.

GKDK Digital Marketing is a freelance digital team of two - not a large agency, and we're not pretending to be one. One of us leads digital marketing and client strategy. The other builds the websites, web apps and mobile apps behind it.

That structure means fewer layers between you and the person actually doing the work. No account managers relaying messages, no junior hand-offs, no inflated overhead. When you talk to us, you're talking to whoever is building or running your campaign.

We're early — and intentionally selective about the projects we take on, so each one gets full attention. What we offer instead of a long track record is direct involvement, clean technical execution, and design work that's held to a premium bar from the first project onward.

HOW WE WORK

A straight line from idea to launch.
